HasNoContracts

contract HasNoContracts

is Ownable

Source: ownership/HasNoContracts.sol
Author: Remco Bloemen <remco@2π.com>

Should contracts (anything Ownable) end up being owned by this contract, it allows the owner of this contract to reclaim ownership of the contracts.
